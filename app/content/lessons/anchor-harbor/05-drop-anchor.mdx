## Lesson 5 — Drop Anchor 🏴‍☠️

You've made it to the final port. Time to prove your voyage on-chain.

Connect your wallet, pick a crew name, and sign a **real devnet transaction**. The program will:

1. Create your **PirateProfile PDA** on Solana devnet
2. Mint a **Metaplex Core NFT** — your Pirate Academy graduation badge

This is not a simulation. Your badge will live on-chain.

> **In Solana terms:** this transaction calls two instructions atomically: `register_pirate` (or skips it if you already have a profile) and `mint_badge`. The asset keypair is generated client-side, and both your wallet and the asset sign the same transaction.
