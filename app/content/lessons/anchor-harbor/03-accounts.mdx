## Lesson 3 — Accounts Structs

The **Accounts struct** is where Anchor's safety lives. Every field is an account, and every `#[account(...)]` constraint is checked automatically before your instruction runs.

```rust
#[derive(Accounts)]
pub struct RegisterPirate<'info> {
    #[account(init,
        seeds = [b"pirate", authority.key().as_ref()],
        bump,
        payer = authority,
        space = 8 + PirateProfile::INIT_SPACE,
    )]
    pub profile: Account<'info, PirateProfile>,
    #[account(mut)]
    pub authority: Signer<'info>,
    pub system_program: Program<'info, System>,
}
```

Fill in both blanks:
- `#[derive(___)]` — the trait that makes this struct an Accounts validator
- `#[account(___,...)]` — the constraint that allocates a new account on-chain

> **In Solana terms:** `init` tells Anchor to call the System Program's `create_account` CPI on your behalf. The `seeds` + `bump` derive a **PDA** — a deterministic address no private key controls.
